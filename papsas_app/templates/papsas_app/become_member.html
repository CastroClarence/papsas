MEMBERSHIP TYPES

REGULAR MEMBER

SAS practitioner currently holding positions covered in the CMO 9s. 2013.



Membership Fee: Php 500.00
Duration: 1 year

SPECIAL MEMBER

SAS practitioner currently holding positions covered in the CMO 9s. 2013.



Membership Fee: Php 800.00
Duration: 2 year

AFFILIATE

Former SAS Practitioner who became officers in the Regional Chapters and National Board for at least 3 years and /or Active members for at least 5 years.

Membership Fee: Php 500.00
Duration: 1 year

LIFETIME

PAPSAS Officers (Regional and National) who have served for at least 9 years or Active Members for at least 15 years.


Membership Fee: Php 5000.00
Duration: 1 year
